# ABOUT THE TRIAL

To measure StimBlue+’s effectiveness for cereals, Sagea, an Independent Research Organization conducted a series of 3 trials across Serbia under standard maintenance conditions (*no fertilization and stressors). StimBlue+ was benchmarked against a control group and a leading biostimulant competitor made of Ecklonia Maxima. The results were evaluated using both quantity and quality processing parameters for barley. The results were significant, showcasing a +30% increase in yield. Read the details below.

# BIGGER GRAINS

* At harvest, plots treated with StimBlue+ consistently showed a higher thousand kernel weight for the different trials, leading to better yield outcomes, more robust plants, and avoidance of over-planting (cost-saving). StimBlue+ at 2L /ha **outperformed the control and competitor by +3%** for the thousand kernel weight, expressed in grams.
